Why girls have to be self independent

Girls, it's essential to be self-independent because:

  • Independence boosts self-esteem and self-confidence.
  • Financial freedom allows you to pursue your dreams and passions.
  • It helps you make your own choices and decisions.
  • You become a role model for others and inspire change.
  • Being self-reliant means you're in control of your destiny.
